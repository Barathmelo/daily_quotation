Privacy Policy

App: Quoteary (DailyQuotation) · Last updated: 2026-06-09

This Privacy Policy describes how the Quoteary app ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it on your Apple device. By using the App you agree to the terms below.

1. Information we do NOT collect

The App does not require account creation and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, microphone, or camera. We do not maintain any server-side user database.

2. Information stored only on your device

  • Quotes you favorite and your reading position — stored locally on your device.
  • Appearance preferences (font, theme) — stored locally on your device.

This data never leaves your device through us. If you have iCloud sync enabled for the App on your Apple ID, Apple may sync it across your own devices under Apple's privacy terms.

3. Subscriptions and purchases

When you purchase an auto-renewable subscription, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store under Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not see your payment details.

Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat, a third-party service that receives an anonymous app-generated identifier and the App Store transaction receipt in order to determine whether your subscription is active. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details. We do not transmit personal identifiers to RevenueCat.

4. Analytics, advertising, tracking

The App contains no third-party analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and does not perform cross-app or cross-website tracking as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

5. Children's privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your choices

  • You can clear all local data by deleting the App from your device.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
